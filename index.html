📌 Tips for Best Results

1

Use a clear, well-lit photo of a single leaf against a plain background.

2

Make sure the disease symptoms are visible — spots, yellowing, or lesions.

3

Only supported plants (Apple, Tomato, Potato etc.) will give accurate results.

4

For unknown plants, use the Manual Search tab instead.
